About Me

I am a Computer Scientist working to advance the understanding and application of artificial intelligence in the domains of computer vision and natural language processing. I aim to apply my research to real-world challenges in healthcare, autonomous systems, and multimedia analysis, and am enthusiastic about interdisciplinary collaborations that bridge computer vision with natural language processing, reinforcement learning, and robotics. Currently, I am a Lecturer at the AI and Data Science Department of FAST National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NUCES), Islamabad.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning: deep learning, transfer learning
  • Computer Vision: face recognition, video analytics, medical image analysis
  • Natural Language Processing: text generation, large language models
